About Us

Founded in 2001 with a capacity of 30 students, Thika Bell House Academy has grown since then to a modern day capacity of 200 students.

Our teachers are always present at the school from 8.00 am to 6.00 pm to ensure enough time has been dedicated to learning and consultations by students.

We are constantly keeping our teaching processes up to date, and this has led to the production of top-performing students.
